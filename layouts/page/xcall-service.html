xCall completes the modular blockchain stack by providing dApps with a
customizable interoperability component.

How it works

Aggregation

xCall integrates with and abstracts away all protocol-specific components of sending a cross-chain message allowing devs to focus on their app and leave cross-chain messaging up to us.

Multi-protocol verification

xCall allows sending messages across one or more cross-chain messaging protocols to mitigate risk associated with any one solution.

Customizable

xCall is fully customizable, allowing application developers to permissionlessly add their own cross-chain messaging solutions while still using the xCall interface.
